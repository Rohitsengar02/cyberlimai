# Routes Mapping

Here are the page routes and their entry points in the project.

## `/`
- **Route file**: `src/app/page.tsx`
- **Component**: `PortfolioLanding` (`src/components/PortfolioLanding.tsx`)
- **Layout**: `RootLayout` (`src/app/layout.tsx`)
- **Description**: Personal portfolio landing page for Rohit with interactive hover reveal effect and background animated waves.

## `/dashboard`
- **Route file**: `src/app/dashboard/page.tsx`
- **Component**: `DashboardPage`
- **Layout**: `LayoutWrapper` (`src/components/LayoutWrapper.tsx`)
- **Description**: Main ERP Overview dashboard showing metrics cards, stats, and charts.

## `/leads-management`
- **Route file**: `src/app/leads-management/page.tsx`
- **Component**: `LeadsManagementPage`
- **Layout**: `LayoutWrapper` (`src/components/LayoutWrapper.tsx`)
- **Description**: CRM pipeline Kanban board, leads table list, and automation options.
